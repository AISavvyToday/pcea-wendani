About Term Transition

This process carries forward student balances from one term to the next:

  • Outstanding Balances: If a student owes money at the end of a term, this becomes their balance_bf_original for the new term.
  • Overpayments: If a student paid more than owed, the excess becomes their prepayment_original (credit) for the new term.
  • Fully Paid: Students with zero balance start the new term with clean slates.
Important: This operation updates student financial records. Always preview changes before executing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Run term transition at the start of a new academic term, after all payments for the previous term have been recorded and before generating new term invoices.

  • Outstanding balance: Stored in balance_bf_original and added to the new term invoice.
  • Overpayment: Stored in prepayment_original and credit_balance to offset new term fees.
  • Fully paid: All balance fields are reset to zero.
